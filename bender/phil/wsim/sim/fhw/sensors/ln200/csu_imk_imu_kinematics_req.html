csu_imk_imu_kinematics_0001

Title/Synopsis

IMK IMU Kinematics Calculate Centripetal Accelerations CSU

System Class

IMK IMU Kinematics Calculate Centripetal Accelerations CSU

Req. Type

Functional

Parent

csc_ln200_0001

Children

None

Priority

High

Requirement Description

 The IMU kinematics CSU shall calculate the centripetal accelerations experienced by the IMU due to rotations of the vehicle body.

Rationale

 Used for calculating outputs of IMU accelerometers.

Verification

Test (csu_imk_imu_kinematics_test_a.m)
